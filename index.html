Hi, I’m Ron,

Welcome to my home on the internet! I’m a research assistant, educator, amateur artist and recent graduate from McMaster University, living in Toronto, Canada. I’m passionate about sharing big ideas and crafting meaningful experiences with audiences across the world.

My background in physiology and programming has taught me to approach problems from different perspectives. In physiology (and often in programming), we debunk the idea that there is a single 'best' solution; we accept a harsher reality where the particular trade-offs and nuances matter. Life is sort of like that. I’d love to share my interests with you, from programming and biotechnology to the health sciences. If that sounds fancy, please tune into some of the projects I’ve been working on. Or if you would like to work with me, feel free to shoot me an email. I am always on the lookout for new professional partnerships and engaging opportunities!

In the evening, I enjoy jogging, getting lost in a new book or playing video games with my passionate group of friends. During the course of quarantine, we’ve tried every activity across the board, from movies to investing. Some of us have recently picked up the new and exciting shooter called Valorant. I’m still learning how to aim, but good thing I know where the heart is (because im in physiology lol).
